    About My App

    This app allows users to translate from English to Latin and back through the use of the Yandex Translate and Wikitionary APIS. It also tracks your recently translated words down in a History page and allows you to save words too.

    About The Author

    Hi, I'm Hali Huang, the creator of this app! As a student currently enrolled in a Latin course, I found the latin translators currently avaliable to be lacking. While many translators would provide you with an accurately translated word, they would tell you very little important information about the word like the declensions it is in or the conjugations of the word. After a year of plugging words between multiple websites, I got the brilliant idea to make the process easier by making an app to do the process for me. So that's we got here. This was the first app I ever made, and I'm pretty happy with the way it turned out. Hope you enjoy!
